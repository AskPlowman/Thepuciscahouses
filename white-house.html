The White House

Perched above the village of Pučišća on Brač island, The White House is a luxury six-bedroom villa offering panoramic Adriatic views, a private heated pool, rooftop jacuzzi, gym, and seamless indoor–outdoor living.

About The White House

A luxury six-bedroom villa above Pučišća with panoramic Adriatic views, private heated pool, jacuzzi, gym, and expansive terraces designed for effortless indoor–outdoor living.

The space +

The White House is a spacious luxury villa above Pučišća on the island of Brač, designed for comfortable group stays with a strong focus on privacy, space, and seamless indoor–outdoor living. Built using traditional Brač stone, the villa blends Mediterranean character with modern comfort across multiple levels.

The layout offers generous communal areas alongside well-separated private spaces, allowing guests to gather together or enjoy quiet moments throughout the house. Large terraces, open-plan living areas, and panoramic views over the Adriatic Sea create a relaxed and natural flow between interior and exterior spaces.

Thoughtful design, quality materials, and elevated positioning above the village ensure both comfort and tranquillity, making The White House an ideal setting for extended family holidays, group retreats, and memorable stays in Pučišća.

Bedrooms & comfort +

The bedrooms are individually and elegantly decorated, offering a peaceful and restful ambiance. Each bedroom has its own uniquely designed ensuite bathroom, a flat-screen TV, and separate air conditioning for personalized comfort. The bathrooms are equipped with hairdryers.

Outdoor areas +

The outdoor areas at The White House are designed for relaxed days in the Mediterranean sun and calm evenings overlooking the Adriatic Sea. Multiple terraces flow naturally between swimming, dining, and lounging, all framed by panoramic sea views across Pučišća on Brač island.

The main terrace features a private heated pool, sunbeds, and shaded seating, while the outdoor kitchen with BBQ and pizza oven makes al fresco dining easy for the whole group.

Above, the sun deck with a large jacuzzi enjoys sun from morning to sunset — the perfect place to unwind while watching Pučišća glow below. Thoughtfully landscaped with native plants and stone details, the outdoor spaces offer privacy, comfort, and a strong connection to the surrounding landscape.
